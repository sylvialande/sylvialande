About:

My name is Melinda Sighisoara, and I first designed jewelry for my family and friends when I was 8-years-old during free time after school. I became good at making bead necklaces with a flower design, that I made in many different colors. Later I went to school to study advanced jewelry-making techniques, and I created new jewelry styles and designs.

I started my jewelery company, Fragaria Vesca Design, in 2017. Fragaria Vesca means Wild Strawberry in Latin. I chose this name for my company because I like the bright color of strawberries!

I sell my jewelry at crafts fairs in New York City. Please find the dates and locations of the craft fairs where you can find me and see my jewelry on the Events section on the Home page.
